Chicken Masala

Chicken Masala is a delicious Indian dish made with chicken, spices, herbs, onion, and tomatoes. It packs a nice punch without being too much, and it's highly recommended anybody add this to their palette.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Plain greek yogurt
  • Red chili powder
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cashew nuts
  • Butter
  • Dried chillies
  • Ketchup
  • Kasuri methi
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients under marinade, and then add chicken.
  2. Mix well, cover, and set aside to marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Once the chicken has been marinated, heat a tablespoon of oil in a cast iron skillet or non-stick pan.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and sear them on each side for 2-3 minutes. Remove them from pan and set aside.
  5. For the gravy, heat 1 tablespoon oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a pan.
  6. Add cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, whole red chilies, and garlic. Saute for 1 minute.
  7. Add onions and saute on high heat for 2-3 minutes until pink.
  8. Add tomatoes and cash nuts. Mix once, reduce the flame to a simmer, cover and cook for 10-15 mins until the tomatoes break down. Turn off the flame and let the mixture cool.
  9. Once cool, transfer to a blender and blend until smooth.
  10. Transfer the mixture back to the pan.
  11. Add chili powder, ketchup, salt, and 1/4 cup water. Bring this to a boil. Reduce the flame to low, and cover and cook for 25 minutes.
  12. Add chicken pieces, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  13. Stir in the kasur methi and fresh cream.
  14. Serve with naan and rice if desired. Enjoy!
